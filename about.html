About Me

I'm an Ecuadorian documentary filmmaker and journalist based in New York City. I focus on the intersections of immigration and the Latino community, arts, and culture.

I have worked as an on-air radio talent and producer for Spanish-speaking media outlets. As a producer for a New York production company, I have worked in the making of documentary and fiction short films, institutional videos, and grant writing.

As a documentarian, I seek to work with a light footprint and capture intimate moments with authenticity. I approach my work with empathy, efficient communication, and creativity.

I recently graduated from Columbia Graduate School of Journalism with honors in documentary filmmaking and documentary thesis production.
