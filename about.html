About Me

I'm an Ecuadorian documentary filmmaker and journalist based in New York City. While I am verstaile and deeply curious, I primarily focus on immigration, the Latino community, as well as arts and culture.

In the past, I was as an on-air radio talent and producer for Spanish-speaking media outlets in the US and Ecuador.

As a producer for a New York based production company, I worked on documentary and fiction short films, institutional videos, and grant applications.

As a documentarian, I enjoy working with a light footprint to capture intimate and raw moments. I approach my work with empathy, efficient communication, and creativity.

I recently graduated from Columbia University's Graduate School of Journalism where I was awarded honors in documentary filmmaking and documentary production.
