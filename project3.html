Bola!

This is a short documentary on a community of Ecuadorian immigrants in Corona, Queens, who keep Ecuavolley alive - a unique sport from their home country. Through a circuit of hidden courts and sports gambling, the community finds solace from the harshness of migration.

  • Roles: Co-Producer, Co-Director, and Translator
  • Produced under La Moutique
  • Cinematographer: Catalina Kulczar
  • Currently in postproduction
